Refund Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

We genuinely care about your experience with OnMe, and we want to be as transparent as possible about how our service works — including when it comes to refunds.

Due to the nature of AI-generated try-ons, all purchases are final and non-refundable. If you have a concern or ran into an issue, please reach out to us at support@onmeai.app — we're always happy to help.

1. Why We're Unable to Offer Refunds

Every try-on you generate on OnMe is powered by large language model (LLM) infrastructure — meaning real compute resources are consumed the moment a generation begins, regardless of whether you end up using the result. These costs are incurred instantly and upstream from us, which is why, unlike a traditional software subscription, we're simply not in a position to reverse them.

We know this isn't what everyone wants to hear, and we don't take it lightly. It's not a policy we chose out of convenience — it's a constraint of how AI inference works at a fundamental level. We appreciate your understanding.

2. How Credits Work

When you subscribe to an OnMe plan, you receive a monthly allotment of credits. Each credit represents a unit of AI compute reserved for your try-ons. Once credits are issued to your account or consumed by a generation, they cannot be reversed, transferred, or refunded — whether used or unused at the end of a billing period.

3. Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time through the billing portal in the OnMe extension. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period, and you'll retain access to any remaining credits until then. Unused credits at cancellation are not refundable or carried over.

4. Billing Errors

While we're unable to refund charges related to credit usage, we will always make it right if something went wrong on our end — such as a duplicate charge or a technical error that prevented your generation from completing. If you believe you were charged incorrectly, please email support@onmeai.app and we'll investigate promptly.

5. Questions or Concerns

We know AI-powered tools are still new to a lot of people, and we're committed to being as fair and helpful as we can within the limits of our infrastructure. If something didn't work the way you expected, please reach out — we'd love to hear from you and do what we can.

Email us at support@onmeai.app. We aim to respond within 2 business days.
