Refund Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

We want you to be satisfied with OnMe. This Refund Policy explains when and how you can request a refund for subscription charges.

To request a refund, email us at support@onme.app with your account email and the reason for your request. We aim to respond within 2 business days.

1. Free Tier

OnMe offers 3 free credits to all new accounts. No payment is required and no refund applies to complimentary credits.

2. Subscription Refunds

14-Day Satisfaction Guarantee

If you subscribe to a paid plan (Starter, Creator, or Pro) and are not satisfied, you may request a full refund within 14 days of your initial subscription purchase. This applies to first-time subscribers only.

Renewal Charges

Monthly renewal charges are generally non-refundable once the billing period has begun and credits for that period have been issued to your account. If you did not intend to renew, contact us within 48 hours of the charge and we will review your request on a case-by-case basis.

Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time through the billing portal accessible from the OnMe extension. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period. You retain access to your remaining credits until that date; credits are not refunded upon cancellation.

3. Exceptions

We may issue refunds or credit adjustments outside the standard policy in the following circumstances:

  • Service outages caused by OnMe that prevented meaningful use during a paid period
  • Duplicate charges due to a billing error
  • Other exceptional circumstances reviewed at our discretion

4. Non-Refundable Items

  • Partially used subscription periods beyond the 14-day guarantee window
  • Credits consumed by generating try-on images
  • Accounts terminated for violation of our Terms of Service

5. Chargebacks

If you initiate a chargeback with your bank or card provider before contacting us, we may suspend your account pending resolution. We encourage you to reach out to us first — we're happy to resolve billing issues quickly.

6. How to Request a Refund

Email support@onme.app with:

  • The email address associated with your OnMe account
  • The date and amount of the charge in question
  • A brief description of the reason for your request

Approved refunds are processed to your original payment method and typically appear within 5–10 business days depending on your card issuer.
