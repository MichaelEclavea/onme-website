Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

The team at OnMe is committed to keeping your information private and handling it with care. This policy explains what we collect, why, and how we protect it.

1. What We Collect

Account Information

When you create an OnMe account, we collect your email address and, if you use Google or Apple sign-in, a token from those providers to authenticate you.

Photos You Upload

Photos you upload are sent securely to our AI pipeline to generate your try-on preview and are not stored anywhere after the preview is complete.

Your Try-On Results

AI-generated try-on images are saved to your personal library so you can access them across sessions. You can delete any result at any time.

Usage Data

We track credit usage and try-on history to operate the service. Payments are handled by Stripe — we never see or store your card details.

2. How We Use It

  • To run the virtual try-on service and your results library
  • To manage your account and credits
  • To process payments via Stripe
  • To keep the service reliable and fix bugs
  • To respond to support requests

We do not use your photos or results to train AI models. We do not sell your data.

3. Who We Share Data With

We only share data with the services needed to run OnMe:

  • Supabase — account and session data
  • Cloudflare — storage for your try-on results
  • fal.ai — AI image generation (images are processed ephemerally and not retained)
  • Stripe — payment processing

4. Security

We use encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), JWT-based authentication, and restricted access to production systems to keep your data safe.

5. Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we'll post the new version here with a revised date. Continued use of OnMe after changes means you're okay with the update.

6. Contact

Questions? Reach us at support@onmeai.app.
