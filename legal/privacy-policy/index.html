Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

OnMe ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the OnMe Chrome extension and our associated services.

By installing or using OnMe, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please uninstall the extension and discontinue use.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an OnMe account, we collect your email address and, if you use Google or Apple sign-in, a token from those providers to authenticate you. This data is stored securely in our authentication system (Supabase).

Photos You Upload

You may upload a personal photo ("user photo") to enable virtual try-on. User photos are stored locally on your device in Chrome's local storage and are never uploaded to our servers. They are used only to generate try-on previews.

Generated Images

When you perform a try-on, the clothing image and your user photo are sent securely to our AI processing pipeline. The AI-generated result is stored in our cloud storage (Cloudflare R2) and linked to your account so you can access your try-on library. Clothing images you select come from the websites you are browsing and are not stored permanently.

Usage and Billing Data

We track credit usage, subscription status, and try-on job history to operate the service. Payment is processed by Stripe; we do not store your full payment card details on our servers.

Automatically Collected Data

We may collect limited technical data such as error logs and API request metadata to maintain service reliability. We do not use tracking pixels or third-party advertising trackers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the virtual try-on service and display your results library
  • To manage your account, credits, and subscription
  • To process payments via Stripe
  • To improve service reliability and debug issues
  • To communicate service updates or support responses

We do not use your personal photos or generated images to train AI models, and we do not sell your data to third parties.

3. Data Sharing

We share data only with the following service providers, solely to operate OnMe:

  • Supabase — account data, session tokens, and job records
  • Cloudflare — cloud storage for generated try-on images and API hosting
  • fal.ai — AI image generation (images are sent ephemerally for processing; fal.ai does not retain them)
  • Stripe — payment processing and subscription management

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

4. Data Retention

Your generated try-on images are retained in your library until you delete them or close your account. User photos stored locally are removed when you clear Chrome's local storage or uninstall the extension. Account data is deleted within 30 days of account closure.

5. Security

We use industry-standard security practices including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), JWT-based authentication, and restricted access to production systems. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy

OnMe is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at support@onmeai.app.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version here with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of OnMe after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@onmeai.app.
