Frequently asked questions

How do I install OnMe?
OnMe is available as a Chrome extension. Install it from the Chrome Web Store, then pin it to your toolbar for easy access. Press Cmd+Shift+Y (Mac) or Ctrl+Shift+Y (Windows) to open the side panel at any time.
How do I try on a piece of clothing?
Browse to any online store or website that displays clothing images. Click on any clothing item to activate OnMe's selection overlay, then confirm your selection. The try-on result appears in the OnMe side panel within seconds.
What is a credit and how many do I get?
Each virtual try-on generation uses one credit. Credits are included with your paid plan and refresh each billing cycle. OnMe offers three plans: Basic ($5/mo), Pro ($10/mo), and Ultimate ($20/mo).
Where is my photo stored?
Your personal photo (the one you upload of yourself) is stored locally in Chrome's storage on your device only — it is never uploaded to our servers. AI-generated try-on images are stored in our secure cloud so you can access your library across sessions.
My try-on result doesn't look right. What can I do?
Results depend on the quality of the clothing image and your photo. For best results: use a clear, well-lit photo of yourself against a plain background, and select clothing images that show the full garment without heavy styling or extreme angles. If a result uses a credit but fails to generate, contact us and we'll restore the credit.
Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes. You can cancel anytime from the billing portal inside the OnMe extension (Account → Manage Subscription). Your plan remains active until the end of the current billing period, after which it reverts to the free tier. See our Refund Policy for details.
How do I request a refund?
Email support@onme.app with your account email and a brief description of the issue. First-time subscribers may request a full refund within 14 days of their initial purchase. See our Refund Policy for full details.
Which browsers does OnMe support?
OnMe is currently available as a Chrome extension (Manifest V3) and works on any Chromium-based browser that supports Chrome extensions. Support for other browsers may be added in the future.
Does OnMe work on all shopping websites?
OnMe works on any website that displays clothing images — it is not limited to specific retailers or platforms. If you encounter a site where the selection overlay doesn't appear, please let us know at support@onme.app.
