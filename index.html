About

What is OnMe?

OnMe is a browser extension that bridges the gap between online browsing and confident purchasing. When you're shopping and see something you like, OnMe lets you select that clothing item directly on the page and see how it looks on you — using a photo you upload.

We built OnMe because returns are frustrating and online shopping still requires a leap of faith. By bringing virtual try-on directly into the browser, we make it effortless to evaluate fit and style before committing to a purchase.

OnMe is available as a Chrome extension and is actively developed and maintained.
